What Sets Us Apart

The pillars that make Lino Press truly unique in New York’s printing landscape.

Union Printer
Certified union shop. All work carries the union bug — required for government contracts, political campaigns, and labor organizations.
MBE Certified
Minority Business Enterprise certification qualifies for city, state, and federal procurement contracts with diversity requirements.
Founded 1994 — 30+ Years
Three decades of printing excellence in Washington Heights. Proven reliability and institutional knowledge that no newcomer can match.
Bilingual English & Spanish
Full service in both languages — design, consultation, and project management. Not just translation, but culturally fluent communication.
Family-Owned — Nuñez Family
Two generations of the Nuñez family — Franklin and Hector — leading with dedication. Every client is treated like family.
