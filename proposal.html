01 — Executive Summary

A New Digital Home
for Lino Press

Lino Press NY has been a cornerstone of Upper Manhattan printing for over 30 years. Founded by Franklin Nuñez in Washington Heights, the company is NYC's first and largest Latino-owned printing firm — serving unions, government agencies, nonprofits, and the community in English and Spanish.

However, the current digital presence does not reflect the caliber of work being delivered. This proposal outlines a premium cinematic website that matches the quality, heritage, and ambition of Lino Press.
